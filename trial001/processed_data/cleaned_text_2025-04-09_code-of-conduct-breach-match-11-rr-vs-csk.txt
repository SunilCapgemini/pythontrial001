
Code of Conduct Breach - Match 11 - RR vs CSK
IPL MEDIA ADVISORY
March 31, 2025
Match 11, RR vs CSK - Code of Conduct
Mr. Riyan Parag, Captain, Rajasthan Royals has been fined after his team maintained a slow over-rate during Match 11 of the TATA Indian Premier League (IPL) 2025 against Chennai Super Kings at the ACA Stadium, Guwahati on 30th March, 2025. 
As this was his team’s first offence of the season under Article 2.22 of the IPL’s Code of Conduct, which pertains to minimum over-rate offences, Parag was fined INR 12 lakhs.
