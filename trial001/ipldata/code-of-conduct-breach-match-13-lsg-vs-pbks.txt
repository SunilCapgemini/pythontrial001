                        <p>
                        <p dir="ltr"><strong>IPL MEDIA ADVISORY</strong></p>
<p dir="ltr"><strong>April 02, 2025</strong></p>
<p dir="ltr"><strong>Code of Conduct Breach &ndash; Match 13: LSG vs PBKS</strong></p>
<p dir="ltr">Digvesh Singh, Bowler, Lucknow Super Giants has been fined 25 per cent of his match fees and has also accumulated One Demerit Point for breaching the IPL Code of Conduct during his team&rsquo;s match against Punjab Kings at the Bharat Ratna Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee Ekana Cricket Stadium, Lucknow on Tuesday.</p>
<p dir="ltr">Digvesh Singh admitted to the Level 1 offence under Article 2.5 and accepted the Match Referee&rsquo;s sanction.&nbsp;</p>
<p dir="ltr">For Level 1 breaches of the Code of Conduct, the Match Referee&rsquo;s decision is final and binding.</p>

        function setCookie(cookieName, cookieValue, expiryDays) {
        const date = new Date();
        //days
        date.setTime(date.getTime() + (expiryDays * 24 * 60 * 60 * 1000));

        //minute
        // date.setTime(date.getTime() + (expiryDays * 60 * 1000));

        const expires = 'expires=' + date.toUTCString();
        document.cookie = cookieName + '=' + cookieValue + ';' + expires + ';path=/';
        }
