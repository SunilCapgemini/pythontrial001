<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The match, a rematch of the TATA IPL 2024 Final, saw SRH captain Pat Cummins opt to bowl first against KKR skipper Ajinkya Rahane. Cummins made an early impact, dismissing Quinton de Kock in the second over, and Mohd. Shami followed suit by removing Sunil Narine in the third.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">However, Rahane and Angkrish Raghuvanshi steadied the ship with a calculated partnership. Rahane, in particular, looked aggressive, and the duo added 81 runs before Rahane was dismissed for 38 (27) by Zeeshan Ansari, who struck with a brilliant reverse sweep dismissal caught by Heinrich Klassen.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Despite Rahane's departure, Raghuvanshi continued his fine innings, reaching his second TATA IPL half-century, his first of the season. But just as KKR seemed to be back on track, Kamindu Mendis, making his debut, dismissed Raghuvanshi for 50 (32), with Harshal Patel taking a brilliant catch.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">At 106/4 in 12.4 overs, KKR needed to rebuild, and Venkatesh Iyer and Rinku Singh took charge. The left-handers went on the attack in the final overs, plundering 78 runs in the last five overs to help KKR post a daunting 200/6 in 20 overs.</span></p>
